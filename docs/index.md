# Sanduhr für Claude

*Hourglass for Claude — watch your usage sand drain.*

A glassmorphism desktop widget that shows your Claude.ai subscription usage
with burn-rate projections, pace markers, and sparklines. Know at a glance
whether you're burning through your limits too fast — or have room to push
harder.

---

## Download

**[Sanduhr.dmg — macOS](https://github.com/estevanhernandez-stack-ed/Sanduhr_f-r_Claude/releases/latest)** — signed, notarized, auto-updating via Sparkle. Requires macOS 14+.

**[Windows build (native Qt)](https://github.com/estevanhernandez-stack-ed/Sanduhr_f-r_Claude/tree/windows-native)** — on the `windows-native` branch.

**[Python single-file original](https://github.com/estevanhernandez-stack-ed/Sanduhr_f-r_Claude)** — `python sanduhr.py`, any OS.
